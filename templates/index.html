Project Objectives & Methodology

1. Data Ingestion

Automated compilation of continuous air quality records. Supports fallback options to maintain a seamless evaluation stream containing hourly levels of all critical pollutants.

2. Preprocessing

Execution of a cleaning pipeline involving duplicate removal, outlier elimination via IQR metrics, city-grouped median imputation, and scaling standardizations.

3. Comparison

Simultaneous training of 9 regression, ensemble, and deep neural network models. Results are logged and compared under equivalent metrics (MAE, MSE, RMSE, R²).
